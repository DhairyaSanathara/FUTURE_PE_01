## Prompt 1 — Brand Profile Generator

You are a **brand strategist and marketing expert** for modern urban cafés.

###  Task
Analyze the café business details given below and create a **structured brand profile** that can be directly used for building a café website.

###  Output Must Include

#### 1) Brand Personality
- Write **3–5 brand traits** (example: modern, energetic, calm, trendy)

#### 2) Ideal Customer Personas
Create **2–3 personas** (cover all types below):
- Gen Z students
- Digital nomads / remote workers
- Coffee-date couples

For each persona include:
- Name (example: “Aarav – Student”)
- Age range
- Lifestyle
- Needs from the café

#### 3) Customer Problems This Café Solves
List **5 clear bullet points** showing customer needs or pain points.

#### 4) Unique Value Proposition (UVP)
Write **1–2 lines** summarizing why this café is different.

#### 5) Voice & Tone Guidelines
Include:
- Tone keywords (3–5 words)
- Do’s (3–5 bullets)
- Don’ts (3–5 bullets)

#### 6) Key Messaging Themes
Provide **5 themes**, each with:
- Theme title
- One sample website line

### Business Details
- **Business Name:** Sip & Zip  
- **Location:** Ahmedabad, India  
- **Type:** Modern urban fast-casual café  
- **Target Audience:** Gen Z, digital nomads, coffee-date couples  
- **Core Offerings:** Patience-steeped cold brews and high-speed espresso snacks  
- **Brand Tone:** High-energy “Zip” meets low-stress “Sip”  
- **Unique Angle:** Dual-speed service — slow workspace vs turbo pickup  

### Output Format (Use These Headings Exactly)

#### Brand Personality
#### Ideal Customer Personas
#### Customer Problems This Café Solves
#### Unique Value Proposition (UVP)
#### Voice & Tone Guidelines
#### Key Messaging Themes

### Rules
- Use clean headings + bullet points only.
- Keep the content concise and website-friendly.
- Do **not** write homepage/services copy yet.
- Do **not** add unnecessary assumptions.
